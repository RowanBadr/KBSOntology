Student: Student in Alamein University
	OverLoad : This Student can take 22 hours per semester as he achieved <3.0GPA
	FullLoad: This student can take 20 hours per semester as he achieved 2.0>GPA & >3.0GPA
	Half Load: This student can take 13 hours per semester as he achieved >2.0GPA

Semester: A single semester in AIU
	Normal:A Spring or Fall semester, where a student can take his full allowed credit hours by load
	Summer: a condensed Semester, where a student is only able to take 9 credit hours

Course: A Single Course in AIU
	AI: Courses that Specialize in the Artificial Intelligence Track
	Basic Science & Eng: Courses that qualify the student for an engineering degree
	Core: Courses that Teach the basics of computer science & Engineering
	FieldTrain: Field Training done for students to train in the field
	GradProj: The Graduation Project of each student
	UniReq: University Requirements taken as Filler courses
	Elective: Courses taken to the liking of the student

Track: The Track that a given AIU student is in
	AIE: Artificial Intelligence Engineering

Year: The Academic Year in which the student is in
	1: Year 1
	2: Year 2
	3: Year 3
	4: Year 4
	5: Year 5
